Our Story

ZISAS Highly Blessed Contractors was founded in 2014 with a vision to transform the construction landscape in Zimbabwe. Based in Kuwadzana, Harare, we have grown from a small local contractor to one of the most trusted construction companies in the region. Our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction has been the cornerstone of our success.

  • Over 10 years of construction excellence in Zimbabwe
  • Licensed and certified construction professionals
  • Modern construction techniques and quality materials

We specialize in residential and commercial construction, offering comprehensive services from design to completion. Our experienced team ensures every project meets the highest standards of quality and safety, delivering results that exceed our clients' expectations.
